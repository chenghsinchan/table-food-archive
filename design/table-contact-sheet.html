The atmosphere stamp

After a photo saves, one optional sheet: drag a dot — energy across, tone up and down. Weather and daypart attach themselves. The dot here sits at drained × soothing: a tired evening that helped. Afterwards it lives as a tiny mark, never a chart.

The image is the object

No cards, borders, or captions in the gallery. Titles, tags and metadata appear only on press or in the detail — the glance stays photographic.

Entries, not dishes

An entry happened once; a dish is reusable. One pho, seven memories. HOME shows entries; SUNDAY and “plan again” reuse the dish underneath.

Motion, not texture

Physicality comes from behaviour: frames swell slightly on press, the detail slides up like a print pulled from a stack, and returns precisely to its slot. No fibres, no tape.

Context without questions

Weather, daypart, and place attach automatically. The only ask is one dot — and even that is skippable. Meaning accrues; nothing is demanded.
